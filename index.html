Akan Name

This is a web application that tells you your Akan name. Akan names are derived fron Ghanian culture where children are given their first name as a 'day name'. these names correspond to the day in the week they were born and vary depending on gender.

Below are the Akan names for both genders:

MALE

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

FEMALE

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
